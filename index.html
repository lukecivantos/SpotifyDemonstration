Spotify Playlists - Song Recommendations

For our project, we opted to build a model that recommends Spotify songs based on a preprocessed dataset of one million playlists. The difficult aspect arises in understanding - how do you score a recommendation?

Our Method:

1. Sample a random playlist of length greater than 10

2. Randomly obscure a quarter of the playlist

3. Using our models, recommend songs

4. Compare to obscured songs - accuracy is ratio of correct predictions
